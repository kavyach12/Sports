About the Sports Department

Key Activities and Facilities

Organizing Sports Events

The department organizes a wide range of sports events, including cricket, volleyball, kho-kho, kabaddi, basketball, tennis, hockey, football, handball, and badminton.

Indoor Facilities

They offer synthetic badminton courts, table tennis, caroms, chess, and a multi-station gymnasium for indoor training and recreation.

Summer Sports Camps

SRKR conducts summer sports camps to encourage children to engage in physical activity from a young age. These camps often focus on specific sports like badminton and chess.

Promoting Sportsmanship

The department emphasizes the importance of discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship through its activities and training programs.

Preparing for Competitions

Students are trained for state, university, and national level competitions, with many achieving success in various events.

Special Events

They participate in and organize events like "Azaadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" basketball competitions, recognizing and rewarding student achievements.

Collaboration

The department collaborates with other departments and organizations, such as the Women Empowerment Cell, to conduct inclusive sports-related activities.
